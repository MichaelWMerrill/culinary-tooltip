Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Empirical BBQ ("we", "us", or "our"), operating the website at empiricalbbq.com and its cooking calculators, handles information when you use the site. Empirical BBQ is a set of client-side calculators; we do not operate user accounts and do not collect names, email addresses, or payment details through this site.

1. Local Browser Storage (localStorage)

Our calculators save your preferences and inputs directly in your browser using localStorage — for example, your saved rig configuration, wrap and pit selections, weights, and other tool settings ("layout saving"). This data is stored only on your device, is never transmitted to us or to any server, and is used solely to remember your setup between visits and across our tools. You can erase it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain, or by using any in-app "reset" controls we provide.

2. Anonymous Usage Analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics)

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate, anonymous usage of the site (such as page views and general performance). Cloudflare Web Analytics is privacy-first: it does not use cookies, does not fingerprint individuals, and does not collect personally identifiable information or track you across other websites. The statistics we see are aggregated and cannot be used to identify you. See Cloudflare Web Analytics for details.

3. Amazon Associate Affiliate Links

Empirical BBQ is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program. Some outbound links on our pages are affiliate links, and as an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases made through them, at no additional cost to you. When you click an affiliate link and visit Amazon, your activity on Amazon is governed by Amazon's Privacy Notice, not this policy. We do not receive your personal purchase details.

We may display advertising served by third-party programmatic ad networks, including Google AdSense. These networks may use cookies — including Google's DART cookie — to serve interest-based ads based on your visits to this and other websites. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to personalize the ads you see.

  • You can opt out of Google's use of advertising cookies by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of interest-based advertising from many participating vendors at aboutads.info and Your Online Choices (EU).
  • You may also disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features that rely on local storage may be affected.

5. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require registration and do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or payment information on this site. We do not sell or rent personal information. The only data tied to you personally lives in your own browser's local storage, as described in Section 1.

This site is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
